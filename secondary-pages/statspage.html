Job Outlook

The employment of web developers/designers is projected to grow by 8% from 2019 to 2020, which is much faster than the average for all occupations.

About 13,400 job openings are projected each year, on average.

The exponential growth of ecommerce has assisted in the growth of the web development/design field. Also, the rise in use of mobile phones has led to increased demand for developers and designers, because of the varying popular screen sizes.
