Education

While you can get a job in web development/design with only a high school diploma, most employers prefer an associate's or bachelor's degree in a relevant field: computer science or programming for developers, web/digital design or graphic design for designers.

Important Skills/Qualities

Web developers specifically need an understanding of at least HTML programming, but many employers will also want understanding of Javascript as well. More general important qualities are as follows:

  • Communication skills
  • Creativity
  • Customer service skills
  • Detail-orientation
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Critical thinking skills
